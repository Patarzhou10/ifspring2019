Acai Bowl

A simple, quick, and nutritous snack for any occasion.

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Calories: 187kcal
  • Servings: 2
  • Author: Patrick Zhou

Ingredients


  • 1 cup of apple juice
  • 1 large banana sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups of frozen berries
  • 1/2 cup vanilla greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 frozen packet of acai berry puree (100g), broken into pieces
  • Assorted toppings such as sliced almonds, berries, etc.


Instructions

1. Place the apple juice, banana, frozen berries, yogurt, honey and acai puree in blender.






2. Blend until thoroughly smooth and pour the smoothie into two deep bowls.






3. Arrange the desired toppings over your smoothie bowls and serve.




ENJOY

Acai Bowl

A healthy snack for light eating

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Calories: 187kcal
  • Servings: 2
  • Author: Patrick Zhou

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of apple juice
  • 1 large banana sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups of frozen berries
  • 1/2 cup vanilla greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 frozen packet of acai berry puree (100g), broken into pieces
  • Assorted toppings such as sliced almonds, berries, etc.


Instructions

Place the apple juice, banana, frozen berries, yogurt, honey and acai puree in the blender.

Blend until thoroughly combined and smooth and pour the smoothie into two deep bowls.

Arrange the desired toppings over your smoothie bowls and serve.
